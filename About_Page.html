Tambulig, Zamboanga Del Sur

Nestled within the picturesque landscapes of the Zamboanga Peninsula, Tambulig in Zamboanga del Sur, Philippines, is a quaint municipality with a rich history and a bounty of natural wonders. If you're seeking an off-the-beaten-path destination that promises tranquility and authentic experiences, Tambulig is the perfect choice for your next adventure. Let's embark on a journey to uncover the hidden charms of this captivating town.

Glimpse into History:

Tambulig's story dates back to December 27, 1957, when it was founded as a budding community in the 1st district of Zamboanga del Sur. Over the years, Tambulig has flourished, becoming a significant cultural and economic hub in the region.

Embracing Nature's Beauty:

Encompassing an area of 130.65 square kilometers, Tambulig is a treasure trove of natural beauty. The landscape varies from rolling hills to fertile plains, creating a breathtaking backdrop for visitors and locals alike.

31 Barangays, One Community:

The municipality is composed of 31 vibrant barangays, each contributing to the unique tapestry of Tambulig's culture and traditions.
