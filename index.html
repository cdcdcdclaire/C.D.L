Vision

TAMBULIG AS A PRODUCTIVE, PEACEFUL AND UNITED COMMUNITY OF GOD-FEARING PEOPLE; HEALTHY, PROGRESSIVE, SELF-RELIANT AND LIVING IN A SUSTAINED ENVIRONMENT-FRIENDLY SOCIETY FULLY UTILIZING ADEQUATE AND WELL-MANAGED PHYSICAL INFRASTRUCTURE AND FACILITIES AND ENJOYING ECONOMY UPLIFTMENT WITH A WELL-DEVELOPED LIVELIHOOD BASED ON AGRICULTURE AND AGRI-CULTURE CENTER; AND WHERE GOVERNANCE IS EFFECTIVE AND EFFECIENT THAT IS GOD-CENTERED AND PRO-PEOPLE.

Mission

IN ORDER TO ENSURE THE WELFARE AND WELL-BEING TO ITS PEOPLE, THE MUNICIPAL GOVERMENT OF TAMBULIG, THEREFORE, COMMIT THEMSELVES TO: PROVIDE BASIC AND ESSENTIAL SERVICES AND UTILITIES; INCREASE AGRICULTURAL PORDUCTION AND ENCOURAGE AGRI-BASED INDUSTRY; THE UPLIFTMENT OF THE QUALITY OF LIFE IN A FAIR, JUST AND HUMANE SOCIETY; AND THE PROTECTION AND CONSERVATIVE OF THE ENVIRONMENT TO SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT.
